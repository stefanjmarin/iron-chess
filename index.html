Asthma management
Keep inhaler nearby • Warm up slowly • Nose-breathe during warm-up • 90 sec rest (longer if needed) • If wheezing: stop, inhaler, rest 5 min • Stay hydrated
Flat feet tips
Wear supportive shoes on leg day • Goblet squats over back squats • Stationary lunges only • Seated overhead press • Consider arch-support insoles
How your Chess Elo works
Your fitness Elo starts at 400, just like a beginner chess player. Here's how you earn and lose points:

GAINS:
+12 Elo for every workout completed
+20 Elo for every personal record (heaviest weight on an exercise)
+3 Elo for each day of your active streak (up to 90 days)
+25 Elo bonus for getting 100% completion in a workout
+30 to +100 Elo milestone bonuses at 10, 25, 50, and 100 workouts

LOSSES:
-30 Elo for every scheduled workout day you skip
-10 Elo for every exercise you don't complete

Title progression:
400+ Pawn ♟ → 600+ Knight ♞ → 800+ Bishop ♝ → 1000+ Rook ♜ → 1200+ Queen ♛ → 1400+ King ♚ → 1600+ Candidate Master → 1800+ National Master → 2000+ FIDE Master → 2200+ International Master → 2500+ Grandmaster

How fast can you reach GM? At 3 workouts/week with zero skips and all exercises done: ~9 months. Skip 1 day? That's -30 Elo — you need 2-3 perfect workouts just to recover. Skip an exercise? -10. There are no shortcuts. Show up, finish everything, chase PRs.

Auto progression: When you hit your target reps for 3 sessions in a row at the same weight, a green ▲ badge appears telling you to increase the weight. Follow it — that's how you get stronger and earn PRs.
